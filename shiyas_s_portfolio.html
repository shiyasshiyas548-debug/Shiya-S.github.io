Final Year Project

Experimental Investigation on Properties of Biodegradable Material Infused PLA in FDM Process

Developed eco-friendly composite 3D printing filaments by integrating natural biodegradable materials, including sisal fiber and wood flour, into PLA. Fabricated three filament compositions using twin-screw extrusion and characterized them for tensile, compressive, flexural, impact, fire-retardation, and water absorption properties following ASTM standards. Printed samples using a Bambu Lab A1 FDM printer.
